Who am I?

Ann Littlefield is a painter. She uses both oils and acrylics as well as worked with other mediums. She is considered both an impressionistic painter and a colorist.

(Mis) Understanding

She paints the world around her not as an exact picture but rather captures the impression and color of what she sees.

Reasons for Abstraction

  • Emotional truth
  • Only way to create something new
  • To be more interesting than a photo
